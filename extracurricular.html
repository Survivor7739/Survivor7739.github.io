The Hidden Genius Project

The Hidden Genius Project trains and mentors black male youth in technology creation, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills to transform their lives and communities. At the Hidden Genius Project I learn how to code, make animations, and make games. We also get taught about how to create a business so we can be successful and we also bond as a brotherhood and stick up and care for each other.
